Found the following results:

  • No significant reduction in the overall rate of POVCH (7/18 (38.8%)), in 18 cases treated with 1.25 mg of bevacizumab within 2 weeks of surgery. When the results are divided into early and late POVCH, 2/18 cases had early POVCH (11.1%) and 5/18 had late POVCH (27.7%).
