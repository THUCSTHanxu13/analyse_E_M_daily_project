Methods

The effect of folic acid administration in the progression of microalbuminuria

Participants

  • Country: Mexico

  • Setting: single centre

Inclusion criteria

  • Type 2 DM patients with 5 to 15 years of diagnosis

  • Microalbuminuria (defined as a urinary ACR between 30 and 300 mg/g)

  • HbA1c ≤ 9% in the last year

Exclusion criteria

  • Acute diabetic complications

  • HbA1c > 9% in the last year

  • Acute infectious process

  • Hepatic disease

  • Thyroid disease

Interventions

  • Oral folic acid: 5 mg

  • Placebo

  • Titration period: NS

Outcomes

Primary outcomes

  • Change in UAE from the baseline

Secondary outcomes

  • Change in serum homocysteine from the baseline

  • Change in thrombomodulin from the baseline

  • Change in von Willebrand factor from the baseline

Notes

  • Study Sponsor: Hospital Universitario Dr. Jose E. Gonzalez & Laboratorios

  • Study has been completed, no results published

ACR ‐ albumin:creatinine ratio; DM ‐ diabetes mellitus; NS ‐ not stated; UAE ‐ urinary albumin excretion
